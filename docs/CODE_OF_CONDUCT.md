# 🌌 SpaceDMV Code of Conduct

SpaceDMV is part of the LEAF Universe — a civilian‑safe, transparent, and inclusive ecosystem.  
This Code of Conduct ensures that all contributors, maintainers, and community members interact respectfully and responsibly.

By participating in this project, you agree to uphold the principles outlined below.

---

## 1. 🌱 Our Pledge

We pledge to create a welcoming, harassment‑free environment for everyone, regardless of:

- Age  
- Body size  
- Disability  
- Ethnicity  
- Gender identity or expression  
- Level of experience  
- Nationality  
- Personal appearance  
- Race  
- Religion  
- Sexual identity or orientation  
- Socioeconomic status  

SpaceDMV is built for **everyone**, from Earth to orbit.

---

## 2. 🤝 Our Standards

### **Positive behaviors include:**
- Demonstrating empathy and kindness  
- Respecting differing viewpoints  
- Giving constructive, actionable feedback  
- Accepting responsibility for mistakes  
- Focusing on what’s best for the project and community  
- Maintaining a professional, mission‑aligned tone  

### **Unacceptable behaviors include:**
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks  
- Trolling, insults, or derogatory comments  
- Sharing private information without consent  
- Disruptive or hostile behavior  
- Introducing harmful, malicious, or unsafe code  
- Advocating for violence, exploitation, or unethical use of SpaceDMV  

SpaceDMV is a **civilian‑first** system — harmful or militarized contributions are strictly prohibited.

---

## 3. 🛰️ Responsibilities of Maintainers

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior  
- Reviewing and moderating contributions  
- Taking corrective action when necessary  
- Removing content or contributors that violate this Code  
- Protecting the integrity and safety of the project  

Maintainers must act fairly, consistently, and without bias.

---

## 4. 🚫 Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- The project repository  
- Issue trackers  
- Pull requests  
- Discussions  
- Community spaces  
- Any interaction representing SpaceDMV or the LEAF Universe  

---

## 5. 🚨 Enforcement

If you witness or experience a violation:

**Do not engage publicly.**  
Report it privately to the maintainers:

**Email:** conduct@spacedmv.leaf  
**Backup:** leaf‑ethics@protonmail.com  

Reports will be handled promptly, respectfully, and confidentially.

---

## 6. ⚖️ Enforcement Guidelines

### **1. Correction**
Minor infractions → gentle reminder of expectations.

### **2. Warning**
More serious or repeated behavior → formal warning.

### **3. Temporary Ban**
Persistent violations → temporary removal from participation.

### **4. Permanent Ban**
Severe misconduct → permanent removal from the project.

---

## 7. 🌍 Commitment to a Safe, Civilian‑Aligned Ecosystem

SpaceDMV is part of a broader vision:  
**technology that empowers, protects, and uplifts humanity — never harms it.**

All contributions must align with:

- Civilian safety  
- Transparency  
- Ethical engineering  
- Non‑militarization  
- Respect for human dignity  

Any contribution that violates these principles will be rejected.

---

## 8. 🙏 Acknowledgment

This Code of Conduct is inspired by the Contributor Covenant (v2.1) and adapted to fit the mission and values of the LEAF Universe.

Thank you for helping build a respectful, inclusive, and visionary community.
